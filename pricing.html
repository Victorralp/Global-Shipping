Frequently Asked Questions

How are shipping costs calculated?

Shipping costs are determined by several factors including weight, dimensions, distance, service level, and any special handling requirements. We use dimensional weight pricing (calculating the space a package occupies in relation to its actual weight) for most shipments.

Do you offer discounts for regular shipments?

Yes, we offer volume discounts for businesses with regular shipping needs. Our Enterprise plan is designed for high-volume shippers, and we can also create custom pricing plans tailored to your specific shipping patterns and requirements.

Are there any hidden fees I should be aware of?

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing. Our quotes include all standard shipping costs, but additional fees may apply for special services like dangerous goods handling, residential delivery, weekend/holiday delivery, or customs duties and taxes for international shipments.

Can I change my plan or service level?

Absolutely! You can upgrade, downgrade, or adjust your service plan at any time. Changes typically take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. Contact our customer service team to discuss the best options for your evolving needs.
